## Required Images

Add these two images to display on your home page:

### 1. Hero Section Photo
**File**: `hero-photo.jpg`
**Location**: `frontend/public/images/hero-photo.jpg`
**Recommended Size**: 800x800px or larger (square aspect ratio)
**Description**: Your main photography work or a professional photo

### 2. About Section Portrait
**File**: `portrait.jpg`
**Location**: `frontend/public/images/portrait.jpg`
**Recommended Size**: 600x800px (3:4 aspect ratio)
**Description**: Your professional portrait photo

- JPG/JPEG (recommended)
- PNG
- WebP (best compression)

## Tips

- Use high-quality images
- Ensure good lighting
- Professional composition
- Compress before uploading
- Test on both light and dark modes
